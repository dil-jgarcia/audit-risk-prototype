How it works

From prototype to insight in an afternoon.

01

Upload your prototype

Paste a Figma link, prototype URL, or upload a screen recording. We support rough wireframes through polished hi-fi flows. No dev work required.

02

We recruit the right testers

Target by 40+ attributes — age, location, device, income, or industry. Our panel of 80,000+ screened participants gets you the right users in hours.

03

Read the results

Watch session recordings, review click heatmaps, and read AI summaries that surface the most important findings — ranked by impact.
